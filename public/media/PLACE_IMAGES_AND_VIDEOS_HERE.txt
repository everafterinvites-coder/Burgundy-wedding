Place all your website images and videos in this folder ('/public/media/').
Once uploaded, they will be accessible in your website under the path `/media/your-file-name.ext`.

For example:
- If you upload 'hero_bg.jpg' to this folder, its URL in the app will be `/media/hero_bg.jpg`
- If you upload 'product_video.mp4', its URL in the app will be `/media/product_video.mp4`

You can use the file explorer on the left-hand side of Google AI Studio to upload files directly into this directory!
